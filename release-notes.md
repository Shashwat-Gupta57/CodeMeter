# CodeMeter v1.0.0

The first stable release of CodeMeter! Measure your code, physically.

## Features
- **TUI Dashboard**: An elegant, mouse-less dashboard to navigate your metrics.
- **Physical Metrics**: See your codebase measured in Burj Khalifas, Eiffel Towers, pages, and trees required to print.
- **Achievements System**: Unlock badges by hitting milestones (e.g. 1 Million Lines of Code).
- **History Tracking**: Keeps a global and project-specific history of your scans.
- **Exports**: Generate stunning PDF, SVG, PNG, Markdown, CSV, and JSON reports directly from the CLI.
- **GraalVM Native Images**: Zero JVM requirements, instant startup.

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed an issue where `cloc` and `scc` scanners would throw exceptions when scanning completely empty directories.

## Supported OS
- Windows (x64)
- macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon)
- Linux (x64)
